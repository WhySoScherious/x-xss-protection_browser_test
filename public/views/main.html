X-XSS-Protection Test
X-XSS-Protection: 0
Disable XSS protection
X-XSS-Protection: 1
Force XSS protection (useful if XSS protection was disabled by the user)
X-XSS-Protection: 1; mode=block
prevent browser (IE8+ and Webkit browsers) to render pages (instead of sanitizing) if a potential XSS reflection (non-persistent) attack is detected.
